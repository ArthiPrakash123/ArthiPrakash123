How to add numbers

Here you will find instructions to use the addition feature of the JPS calculator.

To successfully add numbers, you must know what is addition.

Use this task to add two numbers using the addition feature of the JPS calculator.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Click the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Click the equal button.
The result from the subtraction is displayed.
If you want to add 2 to 3, the equation will look like 2+3=5. The result 5 appears on the screen.
