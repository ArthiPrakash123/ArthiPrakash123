How to divide numbers

To successfully divide two numbers, you must know what is division.

Use this task to divide two numbers using the division feature of the JPS calculator.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Click the division (/) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Click the equal button.
The result from the division is displayed.
If you want to divide 4 by 2, the equation will look like 4/2=2. The result 2 appears on the display screen.
