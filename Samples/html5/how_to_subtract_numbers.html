How to subtract numbers

Here you will find instructions to use the subraction feature of the JPS calculator.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is subtraction.

Use this task to subtract two numbers using the subtraction feature of the JPS calculator.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Click the minus (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Click the equal button.
The result from the subtraction is displayed.
If you want to subtract 2 from 3, the equation will look like 3-2=1. The result 1 appears on the display screen.
