How to multiply numbers

To successfully multiply numbers, you must know what is multiplication.

Use this task to multiply two numbers using the multiplication feature of the JPS calculator.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Click the multiplication (x) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Click the equal button.
The result from the multiplication is displayed.
If you want to multiply 2 and 3, the equation will look like 3x2=6. The result 6 appears on the display screen.
